About the Event

WiDS Paris @ emlyon is independently organized to be part of the mission to increase participation of women in data science and to feature outstanding women doing outstanding work.

Join us for an inspiring in-person event featuring outstanding women discussing their exceptional work in data science, AI, and related fields across a wide range of domains. Everyone is welcome!
